Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Thrives in bright, indirect light but tolerates low light. Water sparingly, allowing soil to dry between waterings. Prefers temperatures above 10°C and benefits from monthly feeding during spring and summer. Wipe leaves occasionally to remove dust.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate lower light conditions. Keep soil consistently moist but not soggy. Ideal temperatures range between 65°F and 80°F. Fertilize every six weeks during spring and summer with a diluted balanced fertilizer.

Source

Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

Grows well in medium to bright light. Water when 50-75% of the soil volume is dry. Prefers temperatures between 60–80°F and benefits from increased humidity. Repot every few years using peat-free multi-purpose compost.

Source

Aloe Vera

Requires sandy, well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight. Water sparingly, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Ideal temperatures range from 55°F to 80°F. Avoid letting leaves touch the soil to prevent rot.

Source

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

Thrives in bright, indirect light but tolerates low light. Water when the top 2 inches of soil are dry. Maintain temperatures above 50°F, preferably between 65-75°F. Fertilize monthly during the growing season.

Source

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Prefers medium to bright indirect light but tolerates low light. Water every 2-3 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Thrives in temperatures between 65°F-85°F and does not require extra humidity.
